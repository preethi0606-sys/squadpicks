How it works

Three steps. Plans get made.

No new app to download. No account for your family. Just add the bot to your Telegram group and start.

1
LINK

Someone pastes a link

Any URL — IMDB, Google Maps, Yelp, Zomato, Eventbrite. The bot reads it and figures out what it is automatically.

2
CARD

Bot posts a pick card

A clean card appears in the chat with the title, details and three vote buttons — right inside Telegram. No link to click, no app to open.

3
VOTE

Everyone taps their vote

Seen/Been · Want to · Not for me. When nobody says skip, the bot flags "Group ok" — your squad can do this together.

4
AUTO

Filmi Craft alerts you

The bot monitors Filmi Craft every Friday. New review posted → automatic card in your group. Zero effort from anyone.
